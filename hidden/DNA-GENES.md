## Top Priority Genes (Most Commonly Researched)

### 1. **MTHFR** (Methylenetetrahydrofolate Reductase)
- **Function**: Methylation, folate metabolism, homocysteine regulation
- **Health Impact**: Cardiovascular health, pregnancy complications, mood disorders, fatigue
- **Common Variants**: C677T, A1298C

### 2. **APOE** (Apolipoprotein E)
- **Function**: Cholesterol transport, lipid metabolism
- **Health Impact**: Alzheimer's disease risk, cardiovascular disease, longevity
- **Common Variants**: ε2, ε3, ε4 alleles

### 3. **FTO** (Fat Mass and Obesity-Associated)
- **Function**: Energy balance, appetite regulation
- **Health Impact**: Obesity risk, type 2 diabetes, metabolic syndrome
- **Common Variants**: rs9939609

### 4. **BRCA1** & **BRCA2** (Breast Cancer Genes)
- **Function**: DNA repair, tumor suppression
- **Health Impact**: Breast, ovarian, and other cancer risks
- **Common Variants**: Multiple pathogenic variants

### 5. **COMT** (Catechol-O-Methyltransferase)
- **Function**: Neurotransmitter breakdown (dopamine, epinephrine, norepinephrine)
- **Health Impact**: Mood, focus, stress response, pain sensitivity, estrogen metabolism
- **Common Variants**: Val158Met (rs4680)
